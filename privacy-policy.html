Privacy Policy

Lucky Lefty · Last updated: March 8, 2026

Lucky Lefty ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Lucky Lefty content posting application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate our Service, including your TikTok account identifier and access tokens provided through TikTok's OAuth2 authorization flow. We do not collect passwords, financial information, or any personal data beyond what TikTok's API provides.

How We Use Your Information

Your TikTok access token is used solely to post video content to your TikTok account on your behalf, as explicitly authorized by you through TikTok's OAuth flow. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on your own device and are never transmitted to external servers controlled by Lucky Lefty. All data processing occurs on your local machine.

TikTok Integration

Our app integrates with TikTok's Content Posting API. By authorizing our app, you grant us permission to post videos to your TikTok account. You may revoke this permission at any time through your TikTok account settings under "Manage app permissions."

Data Retention

We retain your access token only as long as necessary to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy
